About Us

Volunteering teaches us life skills and refreshes our perspective. It’s an experience highly valued by recruiters, student organizations, and other institutions. We’ve experienced firsthand the impact of serving others on our community - whether it's in our hometowns, our university, and even our state.

We’ve all volunteered through various organizations and initiatives, but there has been one obstacle: it can be hard to find unique, meaningful volunteering opportunities. Often, this information is buried under pages and pages of search results on Google, and we get limited to what is advertised on campus, etc. Especially in times like these, the need for volunteers is high, but there’s not much consolidated information about how, when, or where to help. Our website aims to be a hub for all things related to serving the community.

Our website allows users to:

  • Easily search for opportunities that fit their preferences (Location, type, etc)
  • Be notified of upcoming volunteering events
  • Create a volunteering profile that tracks information
  • Find information related to volunteering compiled in one place
